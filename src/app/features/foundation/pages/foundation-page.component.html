What Planforge does

Generation → Verification → Export

  • Physics simulation places rooms as weighted bubbles inside the buildable envelope.
  • Captures are processed through Voronoi clipping, hallway injection, and UV negotiation.
  • Nine verification checks cull layouts that fail frontage, access, deficiency, or geometry rules.
  • Accepted layouts move to construction output: walls, doors, windows, and a typed contract.
  • Contracts are pushed to the Revit HTTP endpoint for preview and apply.
